  • LDL-C = 3.80 mmol/L, BP = 130/80 mmHg, no diabetes

2016 – NSTEMI

  • Coronary angiogram:
    • patent LAD stent
    • 90% stenosis of proximal RCA (new lesion)
    • 40% stenosis of mid RCA
    • 60% stenosis of OM1 branch of LCx unchanged from previous revascularized with DES to RCA
  • Echo: LVEF = 40-45%
  • LDL-C = 2.1 mmol/L, BP = 132/82 mmHg, HbA1c = 6.4%

2018 – NSTEMI
